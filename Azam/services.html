Common Maintenance Questions

How often should I change the oil?

For a vintage Fiat 128 Spider, it's recommended to change the oil every 3 months or 1,000 miles, whichever comes first.

What type of fuel should I use?

Premium unleaded fuel (91 octane or higher) is best for the Fiat 128 Spider's engine.

How do I prevent rust?

Regular washing, waxing, and keeping the car in a dry garage are essential for preventing rust on a vintage Fiat.

Maintenance Tip

Always warm up the engine for a few minutes before driving your vintage Fiat 128 Spider, especially in cold weather.
