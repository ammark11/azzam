Finding My Dream Car

Posted by Azzam on June 15, 2020

After years of searching, I finally found my dream car - a Fiat Spider. It wasn't in the best condition, but I could see its potential.

The car needed a lot of work: engine rebuild, body restoration, and interior refurbishment. But I was up for the challenge!

Here's what I found when I first saw the car:

  • Engine was not running
  • Body had some rust spots
  • Interior was worn but complete
  • All original parts were present

I'm excited to start this restoration journey!

Engine Rebuild Complete

Posted by Azzam on August 20, 2021

After months of work, the engine rebuild is finally complete! It was a challenging but rewarding process.

What we did:

  • Complete disassembly and cleaning
  • New pistons and rings
  • Rebuilt carburetor
  • New timing belt
  • Fresh oil and filters

Hearing the engine start for the first time was an incredible moment. The distinctive Fiat sound brought a huge smile to my face!

Next up: body work and paint.
